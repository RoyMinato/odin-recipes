Traditional Mexican Guacamole Dip

Description

Delicous and fresh traditional Mexican guacamole dip ready to be served with chips.

Enjoy this recipe as an appetizer or snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced (Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until creamy.
  2. Mix tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lemon juice, and jalapeno pepper into mashed avocados until well combined; season with salt and black pepper.

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 177 calories; protein 2.7g; carbohydrates 12.2g; fat 14.9g
